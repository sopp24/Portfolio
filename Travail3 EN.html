Wero : Arrival in France

A few months ago, Paylib gave way to Wero, a new instant transfer and online wallet service. As part of a school project, we were commissioned ( with my colleague Abel M'SOILI) to promote the new application. By creating a campaign booklet and 5 messages (on different channels), we were able to provide complete and professional work.

Compétences développées :

  • Strategic brand repositioning analysis : Understanding the challenges related to the transition from an established brand (Paylib) to a new identity (Wero) in the digital financial services sector.
  • Development of a multichannel communication strategy : Design of a coherent campaign deployed across different channels to maximize the reach and impact of the message.
  • Editorial design : Creation of a structured campaign booklet presenting the strategy, objectives, and various communication elements.
  • Teamwork : Effective collaboration with Abel M'SOILI to achieve project objectives and meet imposed deadlines.
  • Professional project management : Organization and execution of a complete marketing communication project, from the design phase to final delivery.

This mission allowed me to develop practical skills in digital marketing and communication, particularly in the rapidly evolving fintech sector.
