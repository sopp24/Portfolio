Winehouse Promotion Post

As part of my personal music project, I created a classic square-format Instagram post to announce my new album "Winehouse." It features the album cover, the names of the two singles released beforehand, and the logos of the streaming platforms where the album is available. This project allowed me to express my creativity and share my musical universe with a wider audience.

Skills Developed :

  • Strategic Visual Design : Creation of a square-format Instagram post, highlighting the album cover to reinforce the project's visual identity.
  • Release Planning : Showcasing the two singles released beforehand, demonstrating a staggered launch strategy to maximize impact.
  • Digital Distribution Optimization : Integration of streaming platform logos where the album is available, facilitating access for potential listeners.
  • Marketing Communication : Development of promotional content adapted to social media formats, aimed at increasing the project's visibility.
  • Online Presence Development : Utilization of digital channels to establish a direct connection with the audience and stimulate engagement.

This project was an enriching experience, combining artistic creativity, communication strategy, and mastery of digital tools, providing me with a better understanding of music promotion mechanisms and digital marketing in the contemporary music industry.
