Winehouse Amy Post

During the release of my album "Winehouse," the synopsis of the trailer was clear: I climb the stairs of my building and, upon reaching the top, I knock something out (it's not clear whether it's human or not). With the publication of the bonus version "Winehouse Amy", the teaser showed this "thing" waking up and that its remedy is this new project.

Skills Developed :

  • Coherent visual narrative : Cinematic universe with a clear progression.
  • Conceptual storytelling : This approach shows deep reflection on how musical projects connect together in a broader narrative.
  • Creation of plot and suspense : By not fully revealing the nature of the "thing" (human or not), creating tension that encourages the audience to invest in the musical universe.
  • Artistic direction : The design of a visual concept that complements and amplifies the musical experience.
  • Narrative marketing : Use of visuals and trailers to create a promotional campaign that engages the audience beyond simply listening to the music.

This approach is particularly interesting because it creates a conceptual bridge between these two projects, suggesting that the second is a response or necessary continuation of the first.
