Distribution and Creation Support

The Contemporary Music Venues (SMAC for the French acronym) concerned in Bordeaux are Le Krakatoa, L'inconnue, La Rock School Barbey and Le Rocher de Palmer. Through one of the most collective projects of the BUT (entire class), we created data visualizations. With Abel M'SOILI and Tom Martin, we handled the "Distribution and creation support" aspect, with the objective of legitimizing these supports, or even increasing them from the State.

Skills developed :

  • Cultural data analysis : Collection and processing of complex information concerning Contemporary Music Venues (SMAC) in Bordeaux, including Le Krakatoa, L'inconnue, La Rock School Barbey and Le Rocher de Palmer.
  • Creative data visualization : Design and production of visual representations of data to effectively communicate complex information about the music sector.
  • Large-scale collaborative work : Participation in a project involving the entire class, requiring coordination and communication between different teams.
  • Specialized thematic research : Focus on the "Distribution and creation support" axis requiring an in-depth understanding of cultural funding mechanisms. Information resources provided by one of the SMAC directors.
  • Data-based advocacy : Development of arguments based on concrete data to legitimize existing aid and potentially influence public funding policies.

This project allowed you to develop expertise in analyzing and presenting cultural data, while understanding the economic and political issues in the contemporary music sector.
